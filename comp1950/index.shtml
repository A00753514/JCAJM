What is a Methodology?

An organised, documented set of procedures and guidelines for one or more phases of the web development life cycle, such as analysis or design. A step-by-step "cookbook" approach for carrying out the procedure; and an objective set of criteria for determining whether the results of the procedure are of acceptable quality.
More than just Project Management, but it is derived from it.
